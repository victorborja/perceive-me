PerceiveMe

A social experiment about prejudgements

View the Project on GitHub victorborja/perceive-me

PerceiveMe aims to make visible the distance between everyday prejudgements and people's true story, without ignoring that the two are fundamental to an absolute truth. We gather relevant information about people's personalities with a format similar to the Confession Books of the 19th century. Their photos are the starting point for strangers to put in words perceptions about them, like we normally do. Then we procede to confront these tags with the person's answers and invite the judge to add the new perceptions.
